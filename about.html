About

I bring technical thinking into visual work.

I am Martin, a 22-year-old multimedia design student with a background as an IT-supporter. I like working where design, technology, and user experience meet.

My background

My interest in IT started early, and I have always liked understanding how things work. Through IT-support I learned that a solution should not only function, it should also be easy and comfortable for people to use.

How I work

I work independently and structured. I start by understanding the brief and the content, then I move into layout, hierarchy, code, and small improvements until the page feels clear.

What I am learning

The projects help me practice HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, visual hierarchy, and explaining why I made certain choices.

Outside the screen

I have played handball for many years, which has taught me teamwork, communication, and handling pressure. I also spend time on technology and creative projects because I like learning by trying things out.
